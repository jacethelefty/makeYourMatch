Make Your Match

Two Person Memory Game

(From the team that brought you the teacher acclaimed Guess Battle!)

Rules of Play

1. Click the Shuffle Button to Reset Board. (Not Required)

2. Flip over two opposite suit black cards (Player 1) or two opposite suit red (Player 2) cards.

3. If the opposite card numbers match, drag them to your bank, and then take another turn.

4. If they don't match, flip them both back over and pass the turn to your opponent.

5. When the final matches have been made a winner will be declared after your last deposit in the bank!
